YOU’RE NOT JUST PROTECTING A WORKER. YOU’RE PROTECTING A LIFE.

PROTECH INDUSTRIAL SOLUTION, specializes in the supply of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) ranging from head to toe protection, Industrial Safety Equipment, and Fire Extinguishers. Our dedicated focus over the past year has been on delivering high-Qsuality safety products from esteemed international brands like ACME, 3M, Respirex, BATA, Ansell, Pelican, Honeywell, Demron, etc. Our extensive product range covers Safety Helmets, Safety Eyewear, Hearing Protective Equipment, Protective Face Shields, Respiratory Protective Equipment, Industrial Hand Gloves, Industrial Garments, Safety Shoes, Gas Detectors, Spill Kits & Sorbents, Traffic Safety Products, Fire Extinguishers & Hydrants, and a diverse selection of Fall Protection Equipment. Additionally, we provide engineered safety solutions, including anchorage systems, specialized rope access, evacuation and rescue products, and confined space entry/egress systems.
